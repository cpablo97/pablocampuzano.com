Árbol * Área

About the project

The Árbol * Área visualization is a way to understand urban trees and how they change in different localities within the city of Bogotá. The project uses open municipal data to build an interactive visualization that reveals patterns invisible in raw data — like how tree coverage relates to population density, altitude, and urban development.

The visual system maps tree counts and canopy area across Bogotá's 20 localities, allowing comparisons over time and between neighborhoods — making green infrastructure data accessible and meaningful for citizens, planners and researchers alike.
