Uniandes new website

About the project

Design the new website for Uniandes using the newly developed design system. The main challenge was applying the OOUX (Object-Oriented UX) methodology to reorganize the information architecture of the University's web ecosystem, creating a more coherent and user-centered digital experience.

The project required close collaboration between designers, developers and key stakeholders to ensure that the new website reflected the design principles of the design system while meeting the functional needs of over 500 web properties.

Methodology's

We used the OOUX methodology to map out all the objects in the system and their relationships. This approach helped us identify key content types, their attributes and connections — resulting in a cleaner information architecture that mirrors the mental models of the University's users.

Benchmark

We compared leading university websites from across Latin America and globally to understand how top institutions organize their web presence, surface key content, and create consistent visual identities through their design systems.

Search experience

Search is a critical entry point for university websites. We redesigned the search experience to surface the most relevant results — programs, professors, research, and news — with clear hierarchy and easy filtering.

Galleries and the way content is shown

Image galleries and media components required special attention — they needed to be flexible enough to serve many content types while remaining visually consistent with the overall design system.
