CPIT | DEI

About the project

CPIT is a dashboard that allows traffic officers to validate traffic violations and issue fines. The main challenge when designing this interface was to prioritize the information that was relevant to the officer, and that the way they naturally scan the screen matches the way the information is shown.

For this project it was vital to have a close approach to the officers and conduct many observation sessions.
